Supporting the UN Sustainable Development Goals

Our club is committed to advancing several UN SDGs, including:

  • SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth - Exploring sustainable work futures through a degrowth perspective.
  • SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure - Fostering sustainable development through innovation.
  • SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production - Promoting reduction in consumption for a sustainable future.
  • SDG 13: Climate Action - Advocating for climate-positive initiatives and policies.
