Frugal Abundance
The concept of achieving prosperity with minimal consumption, emphasizing quality over quantity.
Jevons Paradox
Increased efficiency in resource use can lead to greater total consumption, a key challenge in sustainable growth.
Green Growth vs. Degrowth
Explores whether economic growth can be truly sustainable or if degrowth is necessary for environmental health.
Pathways to Post-Growth
Discusses sustainable economies by shifting supply, demand, and prioritizing leisure over material possessions.
Social Outcomes of Energy Use
Finds that high well-being can be achieved with minimal energy use, challenging current consumption norms.
