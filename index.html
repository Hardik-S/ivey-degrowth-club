Welcome to the Ivey Degrowth Club

We are dedicated to advocating for a sustainable, equitable, and mindful approach to economic growth, challenging assumptions and fostering critical discussions on pressing global issues.

About Us

The Ivey Degrowth Club provides a platform for open discussions on sustainable alternatives to traditional growth models. Through our Diversity of Thought Series, we explore systems thinking, critical analysis, and multidisciplinary perspectives on sustainability, all to prepare students to lead in an ever-evolving global landscape.

Diversity of Thought Events

Diversity of Thought Week (Jan 6-10, 2025): An immersive week of sustainability-focused workshops, speakers, and reflective sessions. This series challenges students to consider broader perspectives and prepares them to shape a sustainable future.

Join us for events designed to foster genuine critical thinking and align career paths with sustainability goals. Reach out for more information on our upcoming events!
